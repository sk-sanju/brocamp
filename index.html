Meta Verified

A subscription bundle to help you establish your
presence on Instagram, Facebook and Threads.
Click below to subscribe from the Instagram or
Facebook app (iOS/Android) or Facebook web
(www.). Not yet available on Instagram web.

Purchasing Meta Verified on the web is currently
unavailable in some regions.

Establish your presence on
Instagram, Facebook &
Threads*

With Meta Verified, you get verification with a government ID, proactive account
protection, access to direct account support and more. Get these exclusive
benefits on Instagram or Facebook for USD $11.99 when you subscribe on web or
USD $14.99 when you subscribe in the app.

Features, availability and pricing may vary by region.

*When you subscribe to Meta Verified on Instagram, only the verified badge will
also appear on Threads and not the full suite of Meta Verified benefits.

Get started with the verification process. Meta Verified is currently expanding
